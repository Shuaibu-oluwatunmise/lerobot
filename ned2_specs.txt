NED2 SPECIFICATIONS FOR LEROBOT INTEGRATION
============================================

Hardware:
- Model: ned2
- Weight: 7 kg
- Payload: 300g
- Reach: 490mm
- Repeatability: Â±0.5mm

Connection:
- Type: Ethernet TCP/IP
- IP: 192.168.8.143
- Port: 40001
- Library: pyniryo

Motors/Joints:
- Count: 6 joints
- Units: Radians
- Names: ['joint_1', 'joint_2', 'joint_3', 'joint_4', 'joint_5', 'joint_6']
- Types: ['stepper', 'stepper', 'stepper', 'xl430', 'xl430', 'xl330']
- Read command: robot.joints (returns JointsPosition object)
- Write command: robot.move(*joint_list)

Joint Limits (radians):
- Joint 1: min=-2.949, max=2.949
- Joint 2: min=-1.83, max=0.61
- Joint 3: min=-1.34, max=1.57
- Joint 4: min=-2.089, max=2.089
- Joint 5: min=-1.919, max=1.922
- Joint 6: min=-2.53, max=2.53

Joint Speed Limits (rad/s):
- Joint 1: max=0.785
- Joint 2: max=0.5235
- Joint 3: max=0.785
- Joint 4: max=1.57
- Joint 5: max=1.57
- Joint 6: max=1.775

Gripper:
- Type: ToolID.GRIPPER_1
- Control: robot.open_gripper(), robot.close_gripper()

Calibration:
- Method: robot.calibrate_auto()
- Check: robot.need_calibration()

Learning Mode (for manual teaching):
- Enable: robot.set_learning_mode(True)
- Disable: robot.set_learning_mode(False)
